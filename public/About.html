What is FocusNest?

FocusNest is your ultimate productivity companion, built around the proven Pomodoro Technique. Whether you're studying for exams, coding your next project, or tackling important work tasks — FocusNest creates the perfect environment to maximize your focus and maintain sustainable productivity.

Our intelligent timer automatically manages your work-break cycles, so you can stop watching the clock and start achieving your goals. With smart task management and personalized customization, FocusNest adapts to your unique workflow.

Everything you need to stay focused

Smart Pomodoro Timer

  • Automatic cycle management: 25-minute focus sessions followed by 5-minute breaks
  • Intelligent long breaks: After 4 cycles, enjoy a well-deserved 10-minute break
  • Fully customizable: Adjust work, short-break, and long-break durations to match your rhythm
  • One-click Pomodoro mode: Instantly switch to classic 25/5/10 timing

Task Management

  • Integrated task tracking: Add, organize, and check off tasks without leaving your timer
  • Stay organized: Plan what you'll accomplish during each focus session
  • Progress visibility: Watch your task list shrink as your productivity soars

Personalized Workspace

  • Solid color themes: Choose from any color to match your mood or brand
  • Custom gradients: Create beautiful gradient backgrounds for a more dynamic experience
  • Distraction-free design: Clean, minimal interface that keeps you focused on what matters

Smart Settings

  • Gentle notifications: Optional browser alerts to keep you on track
  • Flexible timing: Customize every aspect of your productivity cycles
  • Quick presets: Switch between different timing configurations instantly

What's coming next

We're constantly improving FocusNest based on your feedback. Coming soon: optional user accounts that sync your settings, tasks, and progress across all your devices. Whether you're working from your laptop, tablet, or phone, your productivity environment will always be ready.

Core features will always remain free, with premium add-ons for power users who want advanced analytics, team collaboration features, and enhanced customization options.

Open source & community-driven

FocusNest is proudly open source and available on GitHub. We believe great productivity tools should be accessible to everyone, and we're committed to transparency in how we build and improve FocusNest.

Found a bug? Have a feature idea? Want to contribute code? We welcome all contributions from our community. Your ideas help make FocusNest better for everyone.
