Task priorities

Set priority when adding a task, or change it from the ⋮ menu on any task.

P0 P1 P2

P0 = high, P1 = medium, P2 = low.

Reorder tasks

Drag a task by its kebab menu (⋮) to reorder your list. Order is saved automatically.

Grab the ⋮ on the right of a task and drag it up or down.
